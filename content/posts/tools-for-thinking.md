A partial inventory of things that have changed how I think, not just what I think about.

**Index cards.** The constraint of a physical card forces a single idea per card. When you can't fit the whole argument on one card, you discover it isn't one argument — it's three.

**Talking to people who disagree.** Not to debate. To understand what premise they hold that you don't, and whether that premise is actually wrong.

**Writing for an imagined skeptic.** Not a hostile one — a thoughtful, unconvinced one. This person makes you prove every step instead of waving at it.

See the wiki entry on [[tools for thinking]] for a more structured reference version of this list.
